What's New

  • The H Building

    A new building is coming to Ashwood in semester 2. The new H building for seniors is being built near the entrance to the school. This building has been long awaited since last year when they started construction and now it is about halfway done, excluding the inner decor.


  • The International Student Program

    The International Student Program at Ashwood is the schools way of showing that they care for those that come from other countries by celebrating and respecting their traditions. The celebrations are only for them but it is still a good and kind way for Dr Moore to amp up the schools reputation. By implementing the Internationsal Student Program many more people from other countries have started attending not just in year 7.
